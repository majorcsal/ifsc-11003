Frequently Asked Questions

What are Mars rovers

A Mars rover is a remote-controlled robotic vehicle capable of moving across the Martian terrain. it is powered by solar panels and is designed with six wheel to better cross the terrain of mars

What are the missions of the rovers

the primary mission goals of Mars rovers are to explore Mars geology, climate, and for signs of past life, and prepare for possible future human exploration. This also including collecting samples of mars for possible return to Earth

Why do rovers last longer then expected

Rovers often last far longer then expected do to their robust engineering and are often over engineered. the scientist and engineers who make the rovers also learn from each of the previous ones and imporve upon their current design.

Are there going to be new rovers

Right now currently no. The plan is to send retrivel landers which are not exactly rovers to collect sample from the rovers that are currently on mars
